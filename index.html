Our Story

Founded in 2008 in a small London flat, Folio & Ink grew from a passion for the written word into a beloved independent bookstore. We believe every book carries a universe within, and our mission is to connect readers with stories that transform, challenge, and endure. Our shelves are curated with love — each title hand-selected by our team of devoted readers.
